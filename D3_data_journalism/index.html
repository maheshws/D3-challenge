Correlations Discovered Between Poverty & Lack of Healthcare

When we compare the poverty rates with lack of healthcare percentage points for all 50 states, we notice that the poorer a person is, the more likely he/she lacks healthcare. For poverty rate between 16-17%, Texas ranks highest in lack of healthcare coverage. For poverty rate between 11-12%, Massachusetts ranks lowest in lack of healthcare coverage.
